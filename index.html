Top 3 Movies

Starring Ryan Gosling

Blade Runner 2049

In this visually stunning sequel to the sci-fi classic, Ryan Gosling stars as 'K', a replicant blade runner who uncovers long-buried secrets that could plunge what's left of society into chaos. His quest leads him to track down former blade runner Rick Deckard, leading to profound questions about identity, humanity, and love.

The Gray Man

In this action-packed thriller, Ryan Gosling plays a highly skilled, anonymous CIA operative known as "Six". When he accidentally uncovers dark agency secrets, he becomes a primary target and is hunted across the globe by a former colleague who will stop at nothing to take him down, testing his skills and ingenuity to the limit.

The Big Short:

In this gripping drama based on real events, Ryan Gosling portrays Jared Vennett, a savvy and somewhat flamboyant Wall Street trader. He plays a crucial role in foreseeing the collapse of the housing market and uses this knowledge to bet against the banks for profit, highlighting the moral complexities and repercussions of the 2008 financial crisis.
